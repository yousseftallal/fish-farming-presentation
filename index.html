Introduction

Aquaculture is the fastest-growing food production sector, yet it faces critical challenges in water quality management and disease control. Our Smart Management System integrates modern tech to solve these issues.

The Full Ecosystem

We provide a holistic solution that bridges the gap between biological needs and technological control:

  • Sensors: Real-time monitoring of pH, Temperature, and Oxygen levels.
  • AI Diagnostics: Instant disease identification from simple camera feeds.
  • Automation: Smart relays control aerators and feeders based on sensor data.

Our Motivation

Sustainability

Reducing environmental impact by minimizing waste and optimizing resource usage.

Profitability

Maximizing yield for farmers through data-driven decisions and reduced mortality rates.

Health Seurity

Ensuring healthy fish stocks via early AI detection of bacterial and parasitic infections.

AI Disease Detection

Powered by YOLOv8 Nano

Architecture Choice

We selected YOLOv8 Nano for its balance of speed and accuracy. It allows for inference times <50ms on edge devices, making it suitable for field use without heavy GPU servers.

Training Data

Dataset comprised 5,000+ images augmented with rotations, variable lighting, and water turbidity simulation to match real-world farm conditions.
