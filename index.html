Introduction

Aquaculture is the fastest growing food production sector in the world. Our project introduces a Smart Management System that integrates IoT and AI to revolutionize how fish farms operate.

We aim to solve traditional challenges like water pollution, disease outbreaks, and manual inefficiencies.

Our Motivation

Food Security

Meeting the increasing global demand for fish protein.

Economic Growth

Reducing losses and maximizing profit for farmers.

Disease Control

Preventing mass spread of infections through early detection.

Business Potential

ROI Optimization

25% reduction in feed costs through automated dispensing.

Scalability

Modular system suitable for small ponds to industrial farms.

Data-Driven Decisions

Historical reports helping in prediction and future planning.
