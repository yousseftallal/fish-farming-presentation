Introduction

Aquaculture faces challenges in water quality management and disease control. Our Smart Management System integrates modern tech to solve these issues.

View Solution Details

We provide a complete ecosystem: Sensors for real-time monitoring, AI Diagnostics for early disease detection, and Automation for feed and oxygen regulation.

Our Motivation

Sustainability

Reducing environmental impact by minimizing waste and optimizing resource usage.

Profitability

Maximizing yield for farmers through data-driven decisions and reduced mortality rates.

Health Seurity

Ensuring healthy fish stocks via early AI detection of bacterial and parasitic infections.

Business Impact & Analysis

Traditional Farming Losses
  • 30% Mortality Rate due to undetected diseases and poor water quality.
  • 20% Feed Waste from manual overfeeding, increasing costs and pollution.
  • High Labor Cost requiring 24/7 manual monitoring.
Source: FAO State of World Fisheries 2022
Smart System Benefits
